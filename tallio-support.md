# Tallio Support

Last updated: February 27, 2026

## Contact

For help with Tallio, email: **nathan.d.claus@gmail.com**

Please include:
- Device model (for example, iPhone 14 or Pixel 8)
- OS version (iOS/Android version)
- App version (from store listing)
- Short description of the issue
- Screenshot or screen recording, if available

## Quick Help

- Create a counter: tap `+` when in zoomed-out mode.
- Edit a counter: long-press a card to open the edit menu.
- Change card color: long-press a card, then tap `Change Color`.
- Page navigation and title bar: visible in zoomed-out mode.

## Troubleshooting

- App not updating after install: fully close and reopen the app.
- Sound issues: check device silent mode and media volume.
- UI appears clipped: update to the latest app version and restart.

## Data and Privacy

Tallio stores your counter data locally on your device.  
For privacy details, see the Privacy Policy URL in the app store listing.

## Response Time

Support requests are typically answered within **2 business days**.
